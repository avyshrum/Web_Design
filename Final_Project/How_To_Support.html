How to Support

I am asking you to help support The Ocean Cleanup’s efforts by donating. According to their website, theoceancleanup.com, “As a non-profit foundation, we depend on people like you to help drive the mission and to join us in ridding the oceans of plastic by donating, partnering, or sharing the cleanup progress on social media.” Donations will help to fund research and cleanup efforts! “Every donation, large or small, will help fund the ongoing cleaning operations in the Great Pacific Garbage Patch alongside interception in rivers around the world.” Even if it’s a small donation it could have a big impact.
