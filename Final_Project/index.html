Ocean Pollution - Introduction

I've adapted my recently finished persuasive speech, for Fundementals of Communication, into this website! Enjoy!

We see trash on the side of the road often, but what about trash in the ocean? How bad of a problem is ocean pollution? I’ll be talking about the efforts to rid the oceans of pollution by The Ocean Cleanup organization. I’ve done some research into this organization that I’d like to share. This is important because we should be taking better care of the planet we live on. I'd like to convince you to support this organization’s innovative efforts to rid the oceans of pollution.
