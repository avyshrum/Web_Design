Ocean Pollution

Ocean pollution is a massive issue, a particularly famous area where pollution gathers even has a name, “The Great Pacific Garbage Patch”. According to “The Great Pacific Garbage Patch,” which I accessed on September 21st , 2023, on theoceancleanup.com, a large organization focused on cleaning up the world’s oceans and waterways by developing new technologies, “The mass of the plastic in the Great Pacific Garbage Patch (GPGP) was estimated to be approximately 80,000 tonnes…” The Great Pacific Garbage Patch is one of five plastic accumulation zones in the world’s oceans. Out of these five the Great Pacific Garbage Patch is the largest.
